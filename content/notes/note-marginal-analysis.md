subjectId: managerial-economics
title: Marginal Analysis in Decision-Making
summary: Applying marginal cost and marginal benefit to business decisions.
---
Managerial decisions are optimized when marginal benefit equals marginal cost. A firm should continue an activity, such as producing an extra unit or hiring an extra worker, as long as the marginal benefit exceeds the marginal cost, and stop once they are equal.
