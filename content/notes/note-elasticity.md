subjectId: microeconomics
title: Price Elasticity of Demand
summary: How responsive quantity demanded is to a change in price.
---
Price elasticity of demand measures the percentage change in quantity demanded resulting from a one percent change in price. Demand is elastic when this value exceeds one, inelastic when it is less than one, and unit elastic when it equals one. Elasticity depends on the availability of substitutes, necessity of the good, and time horizon.
