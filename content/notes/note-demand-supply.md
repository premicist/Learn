title: Law of Demand and Supply
summary: The basics of how price relates to quantity demanded and supplied.
---
The law of demand states that, other things remaining constant, the quantity demanded of a good falls as its price rises, and rises as its price falls. The law of supply states the opposite relationship for producers: as price rises, quantity supplied rises. Market equilibrium occurs where the demand and supply curves intersect.
